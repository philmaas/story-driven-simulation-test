IP-based Story Simulations

Philipp Maas, Frank Carey, Chris Wheeler
Fable Studio

Abstract

We present a unique approach to guide the creation of new episodic content of known IP's (Intellectual Property) in a multi-agent simulation. Current generative AI systems such as Stable Diffusion (Image Generator) and ChatGPT (Large Language Model) excel at short-term general tasks through prompt engineering. However, they do not provide contextual guidance or intentionality to the user as part of a long-term creative process which is often essential to producing high-quality creative works, especially in the context of existing IP's. By emulating and sustaining the creative process and guiding the user's intentions in a simulation game we hope to increase the quality of user generated content in playful and intuitive ways.
